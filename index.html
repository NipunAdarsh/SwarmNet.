Process

Three Steps to
Power Discovery

No PhD required. Install the client, set your preferences, and your NPU starts contributing to real scientific research while you sleep.

01

Install & Sign Up

Download the lightweight SwarmNet client. Sign in with Google or GitHub. Choose the scientific cause you care about — protein folding, climate modeling, or disease prediction.

02

Configure Your Contribution

Set when your NPU works — only while sleeping, always in background, or on-demand. Cap resource usage from Light to Max. Your CPU and GPU stay free.

03

Watch the Impact

Your NPU pulls micro-batches, trains locally with LoRA, and sends gradients back. Earn Compute Points, climb leaderboards, and see exactly what your machine solved.

Core Technology

Built on Cutting-Edge Foundations

Enterprise-grade distributed AI infrastructure, packaged into a tool that runs quietly in your system tray.

Federated Learning

Your data never leaves your device. Models train locally, and only lightweight gradient updates are sent to the network. Privacy-first, powered by the Flower framework.

flwr.dev

LoRA Fine-Tuning

Low-Rank Adaptation freezes main model weights and trains tiny adapters. This slashes memory use from gigabytes to megabytes — perfect for consumer NPU hardware.

PEFT / INT8

Direct NPU Targeting

ONNX Runtime execution providers route workloads specifically to your Neural Processing Unit — CoreML, DirectML, or QNN. Your CPU and GPU remain completely untouched.

ONNX Runtime

Sandboxed & Secure

All model weights and training data run inside a strict sandbox. Built with Rust for memory safety. Zero access to your personal files, zero risk of data leakage.
